Mission Statment

At Harmony Haven Care, our mission is to provide compassionate, person-centred support for children and families living with Autism, ADHD, and additional needs. Rooted in lived experience, we are dedicated to creating safe, nurturing environments where every child can grow with confidence, dignity, and joy.

We strive to walk alongside families, offering understanding, patience, and practical care that empowers young people to thrive in their own unique way.

How We Are Effective

Specialist Care. Real Understanding. Lasting Impact.

PERSONAL EXPERIENCE

All our care professionals are fully trained, DBS checked, and regularly supervised. We combine academic knowledge with practical experience to deliver exceptional care.

24/7 ASSISTANCE

We provide round-the-clock support when you need it most. Our emergency response system ensures help is always available for families in crisis.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERTISE

Every care plan is individually tailored, developed in partnership with you and your family. We celebrate neurodiversity and focus on strengths and capabilities.
